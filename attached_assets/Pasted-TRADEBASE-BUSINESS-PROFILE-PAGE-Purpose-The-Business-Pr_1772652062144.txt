TRADEBASE – BUSINESS PROFILE PAGE

Purpose:
The Business Profile page controls company identity, defaults, pricing presets, payment settings, and automation rules. These settings automatically populate quotes, invoices, jobs, and customer records.

==================================================

Website

City
State
Zip Code

Service Area

Signature Block (Used on Quotes/Invoices):

Owner Name
Title
Signature Footer Text

==================================================

Everything in this page should reduce repetitive typing when creating quotes, jobs, and invoices.
