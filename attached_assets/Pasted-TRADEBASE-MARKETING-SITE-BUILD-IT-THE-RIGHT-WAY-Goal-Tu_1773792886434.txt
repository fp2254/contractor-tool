TRADEBASE MARKETING SITE — BUILD IT THE RIGHT WAY

Goal:
Turn the public-facing site into a clean, high-converting SaaS marketing site for contractors.
This site should sell the value of TradeBase clearly and push visitors to:
1. Join the waitlist
2. Start early access
3. Log in to the app

IMPORTANT:
The marketing site is public.
The app itself stays at /app for now.
Do NOT change the app functionality.
Focus only on the marketing side.

==================================================

==================================================

Use the current TradeBase visual style:
