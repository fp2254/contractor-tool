The contractor portfolio is NOT a social media profile and it is NOT a game.

It should feel like a living record of a contractor's career, reputation, and accomplishments. Every completed job, review, certification, milestone, and dollar earned should make the portfolio feel more valuable over time.

The emotional goal is pride, professionalism, trust, and legacy.

I want users to feel the same sense of accomplishment that games create, but through real-world achievements instead of fictional rewards.

The portfolio should answer these questions immediately:

• Who is this contractor?
• Why should I trust them?
• What have they accomplished?
• What type of work do they specialize in?
• How much experience do they have?
• Can I see proof?

The page should feel like an interactive annual report mixed with a premium résumé.
