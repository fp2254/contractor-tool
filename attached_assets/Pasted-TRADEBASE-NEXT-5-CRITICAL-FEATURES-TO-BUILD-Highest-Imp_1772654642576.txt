TRADEBASE — NEXT 5 CRITICAL FEATURES TO BUILD  
(Highest Impact Order for Contractor Workflow)

--------------------------------
1. ONE-TAP QUOTE → JOB → INVOICE FLOW
--------------------------------

Goal: Eliminate re-entering data.

From a Quote screen the user should see:

[Send Quote]  
[Convert to Job]  
[Convert to Invoice]

Behavior:

Convert to Job:
• Creates Job
• Copies quote line items
• Links customer
• Links quote
• Sets status = Scheduled

Convert to Invoice:
• Creates Invoice
• Copies line items
• Copies totals
• Links customer
• Links quote or job

Purpose:
Contractors should NEVER need to recreate the same work twice.


--------------------------------
2. QUOTE BUILDER SPEED MODE
--------------------------------

Goal: Create quotes in under 20 seconds.

Add Service Presets.

Example presets:

Radon Mitigation System — $1200  
Sump Pump Install — $650  
Diagnostic Visit — $150  

When a preset is tapped:

• Adds line item automatically
• Auto-fills price
• Auto-fills quantity

Example quote item:

Service: Radon Mitigation System  
Qty: 1  
Price: $1200  
Subtotal: $1200

This allows extremely fast quoting in the field.


--------------------------------
3. JOB SCHEDULING CALENDAR
--------------------------------

Goal: Visual job planning.

Views:

Day View  
Week View  
Month View  

Jobs appear as blocks or dots.

Example:

● Smith Radon Install  
● Johnson Basement System  

Tapping a job opens the job details.

Future improvements:

• Drag to reschedule
• Assign technician
• Route optimization


--------------------------------
4. SEND QUOTE / INVOICE BY EMAIL
--------------------------------

Goal: Deliver documents instantly to customers.

Buttons needed:

Send Quote  
Send Invoice  

Email example:

Subject:
Your Estimate from TradeBase

Body:

Hi John,

Attached is your estimate for the work discussed.

Please review and let us know if you would like to move forward.

Thanks,
Your Company Name

Attachments:

• Quote PDF
• Invoice PDF

Email service: Resend (already integrated).


--------------------------------
5. PHOTO SYSTEM (JOB DOCUMENTATION)
--------------------------------

Goal: Document work and job conditions.

Photos attach to:

• Jobs
• Leads
• Customers
• Invoices

Common use cases:

Before photos  
After photos  
Basement layout  
Equipment install  
Damage documentation  

Features:

• Take photo with camera
• Upload from gallery
• Fullscreen viewer
• Delete photo
• Grid gallery layout
